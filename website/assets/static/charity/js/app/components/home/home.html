Our Mission

The aim of this website is to allow charities list themselves in an online portal such that anyone looking to donate to a particular cause is able to see the available charities and be able to donate to them directly.

How We Work?

Charities can register themselves and introduce themselves by showing their profiles. People who want to donate can search for charities. They can not only ask for volunteer help, but also donate in kind.
